Digital Signage Settings

How To

Configure Digital Signage

Not all signage feeds are supported by Solstice. Always validate signage playback in a test environment before making it live across your deployment.

  1. In Solstice Dashboard, select the Pod display from the list of Your Solstice Instances.
  2. Go to the Digital Signage tab.
  3. Select the Enable checkbox to interact with the digital signage settings.
  4. Choose a digital signage display mode from the list of options:
  5. In the Source URL field, enter the source URL of the digital signage feed or web content you’d like to display between Solstice sessions.
  6. In the Start After field, select the amount of time you want the digital signage feed to start playing after.
  7. Click Apply and confirm changes if prompted.

Some signage providers require you to register your signage endpoints. Please refer to your signage content providers instructions to complete this process as needed.

 How to Integrate an Appspace Digital Signage Feed

To play an Appspace digital signage feed, the Solstice Pod must have internet access. The Pod must also be connected to a display monitor to complete the device registration in Appspace. See the Solstice + Appspace Digital Signage Integration page for more information.

How to Integrate a Carousel Digital Signage Feed

To play a Carousel Digital Signage feed, the Solstice Pod must have internet access. The Pod also needs to be connected to a display monitor to complete the device registration in Carousel Cloud. See the Solstice + Carousel Digital Signage Integration page for more information.

Validate the Digital Signage Feed
  1. Physically go to the location of the Solstice Pod where you enabled signage.
  2. Confirm the signage feed is visible.
  3. Connect to the Pod with Solstice Conference and share a piece of content.
  4. Disconnect and confirm the signage feed automatically reappears, plays the entire feed, and restarts the feed from the beginning.
 Exit Digital Signage Mode

If you need to exit digital signage mode in order to access the Pod's local configuration panel, you can do so by plugging a USB mouse into the Pod and long-clicking with the left mouse button.
