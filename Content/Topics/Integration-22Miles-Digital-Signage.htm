Solstice + 22MILES Digital Signage

 Download datasheet

Mersive Solstice with 22Miles Digital Signage delivers a powerful collaboration experience that promotes communication and user choice in any workplace or campus. Solstice offers wireless multi-participant content collaboration and seamlessly connects web conferences to the room technology, whether it's in a huddle space, conference room, or transitional area.

And when displays are not in use for collaboration, they can display customizable digital signage including interactive wayfinding, real-time space availability, and fully automated company dashboards for a more unified and immersive experience in any meeting or learning space.

Turning your Solstice Pod into a digital signage media player is as simple as:

  1. Creating digital signage using 22Miles
  2. Adding the 22Miles Digital Signage URL to Solstice
  3. Enjoying 22Miles Digital Signage on Solstice displays

System Requirements

  • 22Miles Web Editor or Publisher Pro
  • Solstice Pod(s) with Enterprise Edition Licenses on 4.0 or later
  • Solstice Cloud Management
  • Display monitor(s) connected to each Pod
  • Internet access through your firewall for:
    • Network ports 80 and 443
