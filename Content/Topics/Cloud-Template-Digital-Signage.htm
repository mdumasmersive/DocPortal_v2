Digital Signage Template

How To

Access This Page
  1. In Solstice Cloud, go to the left sidebar navigation panel and click Manage > Templates > Digital Signage.
 Create a New Digital Signage Template
  1. Click Create New Digital Signage Template.
  2. Enter the Template Name. For example, you could name the template by location (London Office) or descriptively (Mountain Theme).
  3. Click Save.

You can also start by duplicating an existing template and making changes to it. Click on the three dots to the far right of the name of the template you want to start with, then choose Duplicate Template.
