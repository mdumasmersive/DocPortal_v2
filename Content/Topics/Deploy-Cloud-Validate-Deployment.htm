Step 5: Validate Your Deployment

You can validate the functionality of your deployment by going through the following steps.

Step 1: Connect devices to network. Connect end-user devices to one of the networks attached to the Solstice Pod.
   
Step 2: Download the Solstice app. Open a browser on a Windows or macOS laptop and enter the IP address shown on the welcome screen of a Solstice display. Click the Connect button to download the client application. When launched, the application should automatically connect to the Solstice display.
   
Step 3: Verify available devices are showing. Disconnect from the Solstice display in the Solstice client application. The discovery panel should appear with a list of Solstice displays available for connection. If the discovery panel is empty, you may still need to configure SDS.
   
Step 4: Test sharing options. Connect to a Solstice display and test the following sharing options with your Solstice app. If playing a video, you should see about 22-30 fps from a 1080p resolution device, depending on its specs. Audio should be synchronized.
  • Share desktop, or mirror mobile device screen (AirPlay or Android screen mirroring)
  • Share application window (such as PowerPoint or a PDF)
  • Share media files (video files and images)
