View and Manage USB Devices

Solstice Cloud allows you to easily view which Pods in your deployment are connected to which kinds of USB devices, both audio/video devices and other devices, such as input and storage peripherals. Knowing which Pods have connected cameras allows you to then enable Solstice Conference and occupancy counting in the Room Intelligence options for those Pods. User can view USB Devices data in initial deployment of this feature, and management functionality will be coming soon.

How To

View USB Devices Summary Data

Solstice Cloud reports the number and type of USB devices connected to the Solstice Pods in your organization, both online and offline, as well as details about specific USB devices that have been used on Pods in your organization.

  1. In the left sidebar menu of Solstice Cloud, go to Manage > USB Devices. You will see three boxes containing summary data at the top of the page.
    • The summary box on the left reports the number and type of USB devices connected to Pods in your organization that are currently online. Solstice categorizes these devices into A/V (audio/video) and Non-A/V types.
    • The circle chart in the Device Status box gives an at-a-glance summary of online/offline status for all USB devices that have been plugged into both online and offline Pods in your organization.
    • The Device Model box summarizes the numbers of specific USB devices you have connected to your Pods in both a list and a handy graph. This makes it easy to see which devices you have the most of in your Solstice deployment.
  2. The USB Plug Event History graph shows a summary of all the times USB devices have been plugged in to or unplugged from your organization's Pods during the last 30 days.
 Use USB Devices Table Details

The Devices table at the bottom of the USB Devices page provides more details about each of the USB devices that have been connected to the Solstice Pods in your organization.

  1. In the left sidebar menu of Solstice Cloud, go to Manage > USB Devices. The Devices table appears at the bottom of the page.
  2. To sort USB Devices data by column values, click the icon in the desired column header row:
    • By default, the table is sorted alphabetically by the Pod name in the Pod column.
    • Sort by USB Device to alphabetically group similarly named USB devices together.
    • Sorting by USB Status will sort the devices list by online/offline status. A red dot indicates the USB device is offline. Hovering over a red or green status dot displays the status in text format.
    • The Since column reflects the date and time when the USB device was last plugged in to the Pod.
    • The Conference Ready column shows whether the Pod a particular USB device is connected to meets the hardware, software, and configuration requirements for Solstice Conference functionality. A green checkindicates all requirements have been met. Hover over the status to see which Solstice Conference requirements have not yet been met.
    • Similarly, the Occupancy Ready column reflects if the Pod a USB device is connected to meets the requirements to report conference space occupancy data in the Analytics information available in Solstice Cloud. A green checkindicates all requirements have been met. Hover over the status to see which requirements for occupancy counting have not yet been met.

    • No camera data such as video or audio captures ever leave the Pod. All processing occurs locally and only the aggregated occupancy count is sent to your Solstice Cloud account.

 Filter for A/V or Non-A/V Devices

The USB Device data provided in Solstice Cloud can be filtered by type of USB device, either audio/video (A/V) or non-A/V devices, such as input and storage peripherals.

  1. In the left sidebar menu of Solstice Cloud, go to Manage > USB Devices.
  2. In the left summary box that lists the number of USB Devices Online, click one of the two links at the bottom that list the number of A/V devices or Non-A/V devices.
  3. A button for the selected filter will appear under the page title, to the right of .
  4. Click in the filter button to clear a selected filter and display more general USB devices data.
 Filter USB Devices by Pod Categories

The Manage > USB Device data in Solstice Cloud (accessible from the left sidebar menu) can also be filtered by any category defined for the Solstice Pods in your organization.
