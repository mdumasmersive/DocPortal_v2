Digital Signage Integrations

With its open design, Solstice integrates seamlessly with other technologies to maximize meeting productivity and maximize ROI of meeting real estate and infrastructure. Because Solstice is a room platform, it can also stream digital signage feeds when it is not being used for wireless collaboration. Digital signage enhances the value of meeting and learning spaces by showing important messages, special instructions, and other communications on the room display in between collaboration sessions.
