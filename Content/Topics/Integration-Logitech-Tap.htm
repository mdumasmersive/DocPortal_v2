Solstice + Logitech Tap

 Download datasheet

 

The Logitech Tap provides a plug-and-play solution to extend Mersive Solstice’s in-room collaboration experience to remote participants with Microsoft Teams, Zoom, or Google Hangouts Meet. Users can start collaborating with Solstice the moment they walk into a meeting space and seamlessly invite remote participants to join via the conferencing service of choice on the Logitech Tap.

Key Capabilities

  • Start your Microsoft Teams, Zoom Rooms, or Google Hangouts Meet meeting with one-touch join
  • Wirelessly share content in-room from Windows, Mac, iOS, and Android devices
  • Unify in-room wireless collaboration with remote video conferencing
  • Optional: Logitech Rally and Logitech MeetUp offer complete room video and audio support

System Requirements

  • Solstice Pod
  • Logitech Tap
  • Room PC running Zoom Rooms, Microsoft Teams Rooms, or Google Hangouts Meet
  • Logitech Rally, MeetUp, or other compatible AV devices (optional)
  • Internet access via Ethernet with DHCP enabled
  • Display or projector with HDMI input
  • HDMI cable x2, Logitech Strong 10m USB-A to USB-C cable (included with most Logitech AV devices)
  • Optional: Logitech Tap mounts (various), Logitech Strong 25m USB-A to USB-C cable

How to Set Up a Solstice + Logitech Tap Conferencing System

Follow the instructions below to set up the Logitech Tap, connect the Solstice Pod, and configure the conferencing system.

  1. Connect the Logitech Tap to the conference control.
  2. Connect the PC running your conferencing service to both power and the network.
  3. Connect the PC running your conferencing service to the room display.
  4. If you have a conference camera such as Logitech Rally or MeetUp ConferenceCam, you can connect it to the PC running your conferencing service via USB.
  5. Attach the Solstice Pod to power and the local Ethernet and/or wireless network (PoE+ supported).
  6. Connect the Solstice Pod’s HDMI output (outer HDMI 1 port) to the Tap’s HDMI input.
  7. Optional: Connect an HDMI cable from Solstice Pod’s HDMI in port and place the other end of the cable in a place that can be accessed by meeting participants for wired screen sharing.
  8. If required, use the Tap to configure the conferencing service to automatically share Solstice’s HDMI connection when the meeting begins.
